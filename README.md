# Reflective Lens

**See your coaching clearly.**

Reflective Lens is a reflection app for football coaches and players. It helps you
think clearly about your sessions and games, in your own words, and reflects them
back to you. It never grades you.

> **Mirror, not verdict.** The app organises what you noticed and gently helps you
> reflect. It does not judge you or your players.

---

## What it does

Two separate, private journeys in one app. Everything you say can be a **voice note
or typed**, and it all comes back in **your own words**.

### Coach

- Set up your clubs, teams and squads. One account can hold several clubs.
- Start a **training session, match, tournament or other**, with its intent: a
  focus, a purpose, and the things you are **hoping to see**.
- **Pick your squad** for a match (starters and game changers) or simply take
  **attendance** for training.
- **Record the match**: the score, home or away, player of the match, and who
  scored, assisted, was booked, or kept a clean sheet.
- **Capture notes** as they happen, by voice or text, before, during or after a
  session, or as a passing thought.
- **Reflect** in your own words. The app asks for a little more only where a note
  is thin, using open questions grounded in England Football coaching practice and
  rephrased in your voice.
- Get a **report** that restates only what you and your notes actually said,
  reviews what you hoped to see, and notes what to look at next. Export it as a PDF.

### Player

Completely separate from any coach, and private to you.

- **Log your own game**: the positions you played, whether you started or came on
  as a game changer, the score, your goals and assists.
- Say **what the game was like for you**, by voice or text.
- Answer **open questions drawn from your own words**, including reflecting on
  anything your coach said to you.
- Build a **story across the season**: weekly, monthly and season summaries of your
  own reflections.

### It learns from you

The more you use it, the more it sounds like you and the sharper its prompts. It
learns your language, spots the patterns in your notes over time, and quietly
brings them back into your next reflection. All grounded in England Football
coaching practice, never taught back at you.

## What it looks like

Open **[the visual walkthrough](web/public/walkthrough.html)** for both journeys,
step by step, plus example reports. It is a single self-contained page: download it
and open it in any browser, or view it live at `your-domain/walkthrough.html` once
the app is running.

The walkthrough covers the full coach flow (sign in, set the intent, pick the
squad, record the result, capture notes, reflect, report) and the full player flow
(log a game, reflect, focus, and your season story), in the app's real design.

---

## Principles

- **Mirror, not verdict.** It reflects and organises. It never grades.
- **Your words, your voice.** Everything can be spoken or typed, and replies come
  back in your language.
- **Private by default.** Coach and player reflections never mix, and nothing is
  shared unless you export a PDF yourself.
- **Grounded.** The reflective prompts draw on England Football coaching practice.
